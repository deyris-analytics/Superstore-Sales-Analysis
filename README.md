# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs an Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail Superstore dataset using Python.  
The goal is to identify key business insights such as top-performing categories, subcategories, regional performance, and sales trends over time.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total sales performance
- Identify top product categories and subcategories
- Evaluate sales distribution by region
- Study sales trends over time
- Perform basic data cleaning and transformation
- Generate business insights from data

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains retail transaction data including:
- Order Date
- Ship Date
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Region
- Sales
- Customer Information

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning
The following steps were performed:
- Removed duplicate records
- Handled missing values in Postal Code
- Converted date columns to datetime format
- Created new feature: `Year` from Order Date

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

### 1. Sales by Category
- Technology is the leading category in total sales.
- All categories show relatively balanced distribution.

### 2. Sales by Sub-Category
- Phones and Chairs are the top-performing subcategories.
- These represent a significant portion of total revenue.

### 3. Sales Trend Over Time
- Sales show a consistent growth trend starting from 2016.
- Indicates business expansion over time.

### 4. Sales by Region
- The West region has the highest sales.
- Other regions show lower but stable performance.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Technology is the main revenue driver (~36% of total sales).
- Phones and Chairs are the most profitable subcategories.
- Sales have increased steadily since 2016.
- The West region dominates overall sales performance.
- The business shows a balanced distribution across categories, reducing dependency risk.

---

## 📊 Visualizations
The project includes the following visualizations:
- Bar chart: Sales by Category
- Bar chart: Top Sub-Categories
- Line chart: Sales over Time
- Bar chart: Sales by Region

---

## 🚀 Conclusion
This analysis shows that the Superstore business has a stable and growing sales structure.  
Key revenue comes from Technology products, especially Phones, and the business demonstrates positive growth trends over time.
